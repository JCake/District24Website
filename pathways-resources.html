Other FAQS:

  1. I am a club officer and want to recognize a member for earning a level in their path.  How do I print a certificate of their accomplishment?  Answer:  Follow the steps provided in our Public Downloads > Pathways Help > Printing_a_Member_Certificate.pdf
  2. My club doesn't meet every week and yet I keep getting these weekly emails labeled "Your weekly Pathways summary”.  How can I reduce the frequency of these emails?  Answer:  Unfortunately, you cannot currently reduce the frequency, but you can turn off these emails completely by turning off the option of "I would like to receive emails related to my Toastmasters education progress and completion" in your privacy settings.  Full steps are provided in our Public Downloads > Pathways Help > How_to_turn_Pathways_progress_emails_off_or_on.pdf
